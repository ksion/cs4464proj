CS 4464 Project : Assignment #3

categorizing food stuffs

Our goal was to illustrate how natural language processing can help people better understand content in context of big ideas. On this site, we imported three food recipe sites' rss feeds to analyze. The to make the aggregate content relatable and useful to the user, we used natural language processing tools to categorize the recipies in terms of breakfast, lunch and dinner. These are common things one looks for while flipping through a recipie book.
